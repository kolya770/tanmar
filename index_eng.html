Company TANmar

"TANmar Ltd " Company is registered in Nikolaev, Ukraine on the 30th of January in 2002, the form of Company ownership is Limited liability company. The Company has got the License of Ukrainian Ministry of Labour and Social Policy which gives the Company the right on agent services of Ukrainian citizens employment abroad. This License gives the Company the right on specialists employment in shipping, shipbuilding , fishing, fish farming, building and other lines of business.

Our partners:

OCEANIC FISHERIES (Latvia), BALTLANTA (Lithuania), TRAWL FLEET (Lithuania), INOK N.V (Belgium), INOK KARELIA Ltd (Russia), Alliance Marine (Lithuania), Pescatlanta (Lithuania), Rio Mar Pescadores S.A (Lithuania).

To shipowners

Merchant Fleet

"TANmar Ltd " Company possesses a large database of marine specialists trained in Higher and Secondary educational institutions in Odessa, Nikolaev, Kherson and all of them are high qualified and have a good work experience on different types of the vessels of foreign Shipowners. Specialists: navigators, engine and deck crew, service staff pass the testing in the specialty at the independent certified Training Centers and also at the State Qualification Commission for Seafarers' Certification. Knowledge of English is compulsory for all candidates. Every candidate shall provide reference and promotion from the last Contract. All our specialists live in the Black Sea region: Nikolaev, Odessa, Kherson, Izmail, Belgorod-Dnestrovskiy. This fact helps us to recruit the crew as soon as possible. "TANmar Ltd " Company has its experienced agents in other Ukrainian ports as well , which also allows to work effectively in the selection of candidates. When fulfilling the request for the crew recruitment , we are guided first of all by the requirements, standards and wishes of the Shipowner. As a rule, for almost any type of vessel we select 2-3 candidates for one vacant position and represent them for consideration and approval of the Shipowner.

Fishing Fleet

"TANmar Ltd " Company provides the crew recruitment service for all types of fishing vessels and fish processing vessels. Specialists of fishing industry registered in the database of our crewing company have training in specialized educational institutions: Belgorod-Dnestrovskiy College of Fishing Industry and Kerch College of Fishing Industry, Kherson Marine School of Fishing Industry and Odessa Marine School of Fishing Industry. Every five years our specialists pass retraining and updating knowledge in certified Training Centers. Our fishing fleet candidates have good work experience in North, South and Central-Eastern Atlantic, the Indian Ocean, Southeast Africa and the Pacific Ocean. The Director and Managers of "TANmar Ltd " Company has their own personal work experience in the fishing industry in the large Far Eastern company in the positions of the Director on Foreign Economic Relations and also as crew members of fishing vessels from 1983 to 2000, which makes our company possible to work effectively in the selection of candidates. When fulfilling the request for the crew recruitment , we are guided first of all by the requirements, standards and wishes of the Shipowner.
